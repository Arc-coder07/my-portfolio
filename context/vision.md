## Why This Portfolio Exists

This is NOT a resume website.  
This is NOT a collection of project cards.  
This is NOT "just another developer portfolio."

This portfolio exists to function as:

1. **A Product Showcase** — Every project presented like a mini startup launch page
2. **A Technical Case Study Archive** — Engineering depth that proves capability
3. **A Credibility Engine** — Architecture diagrams, system design, engineering decisions
4. **A Client Acquisition Page** — Converts freelance prospects through demonstrated expertise
5. **A Personal Brand Website** — Memorable identity in the AI + product engineering space

1. **Recruiters** at tech companies, AI startups, and SaaS companies
